Sprucewood Shores Estate

Sprucewood Shores Estate Winery, a family run business, opened its doors November 1st 2006 in the quickly growing viticulture region located on the North Shores of Lake Erie. Situated within 52 acres of land, the event facility was designed in a beautiful Tuscan style "old world" concept, with extensive balconies that overlook breathtaking views of both the vineyard and Lake Erie.

Sprucewood Shores is located an approximate 35 minute drive from downtown Windsor, and 40 minute drive from Leamington, Ontario. Shuttle services will be provided to and from Sprucewood Shores. See ‘Shuttle’ page for more information.
