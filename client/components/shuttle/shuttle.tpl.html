Shuttle

We want guests to have a good time! This means getting back safely when the party is over. For those who are interested, we will be providing a shuttle to get you to the wedding, and back downtown at the end of the night. The shuttle will be departing from, and returning to, the Best Western Plus Waterfront Hotel. The shuttle is not limited to those staying in the Best Western so please consider this transportation option if it suits your chosen accommodations.

To help us with booking the right size and number of shuttle buses, kindly indicate your interest in this option on the RSVP page. No need to let us know which shuttle time you would be taking, just let us know if you are interested in the option.
